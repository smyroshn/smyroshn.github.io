About me

In a few words

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics & Statistics at University of the Fraser Valley. My research interests lie mainly in Convex and Discrete Geometry, Geometric Functional Analysis with related topics of Probability Theory, Harmonic Analysis, and Information Theory.
